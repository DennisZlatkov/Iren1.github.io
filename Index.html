Points to consider

Creating a furry persona is a creative and fun way to express yourself, but it also requires a lot of research, design, and development. You need to make sure that your persona is original, memorable, and relatable to your audience. You also need to give your persona a personality, a backstory, and a voice that will make them stand out from other furry characters online.

Making viral content is not easy or predictable. There are many factors that influence the virality of a video or a post, such as the platform, the algorithm, the timing, the trends, the quality, the emotion, the message, and the audience. You need to experiment with different types of content, formats, styles, and topics to see what works best for you and your audience. You also need to monitor your analytics and feedback to see what resonates with them and what doesn’t.

Building a loyal fanbase and getting supporters is a long-term process that requires consistency, authenticity, and value. You need to create content that not only entertains your audience, but also educates them, inspires them, or helps them in some way. You need to interact with your audience regularly, respond to their comments and questions, ask for their opinions and suggestions, and thank them for their support. You also need to offer them some incentives or rewards for supporting you financially, such as exclusive content, shoutouts, merchandise, or access to behind-the-scenes.

Animation shorts: These are longer animations that tell a story, convey a message, or express an emotion. They can be humorous, dramatic, romantic, or anything else you want. Animation tutorials: These are educational videos that teach your audience how to do something related to animation, such as how to draw, animate, edit, or use certain software or tools. Animation challenges: These are videos where you try to do something difficult or fun related to animation, such as animating in a different style, using a different software or tool, animating with a time limit or a random prompt, etc. You can also challenge yourself to animate something that you are not familiar with or comfortable with, such as horror, action, or realism.

Animation livestreams: These are live videos where you interact with your audience in real time while you work on your animations.
